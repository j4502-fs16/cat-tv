Welcome to Watch On Demand!  Right here in your browser, you can watch any show from CAT's local program archive (2004-present).

Simply choose the program or producer from the drop-down menu, click on the episode you want to watch and hit play!  If you don't see the show or producer you're looking for, try the 'CAT Grab Bag'!
